How to play

  • Clear all the tiles while avoiding mines
  • A cleared tile has a number indicating how many of the eight adjacent tiles are mined. No number means none of the adjacent tiles are mined
  • Work incrementally from the edge of the tiles you have cleared, deducing which tiles are safe based on the arrangement of numbers

Mouse commands

  • Click: Clears the selected tile, and adjacent tiles if mine count is zero
  • ‚áß + click on a covered tile: Toggle flags to mark potential mines. For safety, clicking on a flagged tile will not clear it
  • ‚áß + click on a cleared tile: Flags all adjacent covered tiles, if the number of remaining covered tiles matches the number on the tile clicked
  • ‚å• + click or ‚åò + click: Shortcut to clear a tile and all its non-flagged neighbors. For safety, does nothing if any "?" flags are nearby, or if the number of flagged neighbors does match the number on the clicked tile
